Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 2025

PatchPush (the "Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Service, you consent to the terms below.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the SMS notification service:

  • Phone number: Users provide a mobile number to receive verification codes and update alerts.
  • Subscription data: Which games a user has subscribed to receive updates for.
  • Usage logs: System logs and delivery events for debugging and support.

How We Use Your Information

We use the phone number to:

  • Send a one-time verification SMS to confirm number ownership.
  • Send the game update notifications you explicitly subscribe to.
  • Process opt-out requests (e.g., when you reply STOP).

Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share phone numbers and message content with third-party SMS providers (such as Twilio or other messaging gateways) solely to deliver the messages you requested.

Retention & Removal

We retain subscription and verification records only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may unsubscribe at any time by replying STOP to any message; that will remove you from message delivery for that phone number.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the data in our systems (encryption in transit, principle of least privilege for access). However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

If you no longer want to receive notifications, reply STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us at support@patchpush.info.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email: support@patchpush.info.
